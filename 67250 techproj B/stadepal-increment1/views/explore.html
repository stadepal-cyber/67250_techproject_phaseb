MonoMuse is a contemporary museum centered on the relationship between art, technology, and human experience. Through immersive exhibitions and interactive installations, the museum invites visitors to explore how digital tools are shaping creativity and culture.

MonoMuse serves students, families, local residents, tourists, artists, and anyone curious about innovation in visual culture. The museum is designed to be welcoming for both first-time guests and returning members.

Founded in 2018, MonoMuse was created to make contemporary digital art more accessible, engaging, and interactive for a broad public audience.

  • 2018 - MonoMuse opens as a new contemporary museum
  • 2020 - Launches its first immersive projection exhibition
  • 2023 - Expands student programming and community workshops
  • 2026 - Introduces a redesigned visitor-centered website experience
